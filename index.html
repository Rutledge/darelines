Darelines Safety & Weather Standards

Your safety is our absolute priority. Please review our safety protocols and weather standards.

Weather Minimums for Safe Flight

Wind Speed: Maximum 15 knots sustained

Visibility: Minimum 5 statute miles

Cloud Ceiling: Minimum 2,000 feet AGL

Weather Phenomena: No thunderstorms, icing, or severe turbulence

Weather forecasts sourced from NOAA Aviation Weather Center TAF reports for your selected date/time. Final go/no-go decisions are always made by the pilot-in-command based on current conditions.
